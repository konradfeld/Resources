Section 1: Pre-Field Checklist

Objective

To provide a comprehensive, actionable checklist for both mobilizers and team members to ensure all spiritual, administrative, and logistical preparations are completed effectively before departure.

Guide to Using This Checklist

Proper preparation is an act of stewardship. This section provides a timeline to guide the mobilization process, which should begin 4-6 months before the trip. The Team Leader should use their checklist to manage the overall process, setting clear deadlines that correspond with this timeline. Regular pre-trip meetings are essential for keeping everyone on track.

Mobilizer / Team Leader Checklist

Phase 1: Foundation (6-12 Months Before Departure)

  • [ ] Establish the trip's purpose, dates, and budget (per-person cost).
  • [ ] Finalize and distribute the team member application.
  • [ ] Begin promoting the trip and recruiting potential team members.
  • [ ] As applications come in, conduct interviews and check references.
  • [ ] Set the schedule for all mandatory pre-trip training meetings.

Phase 2: Building (3-6 Months Before Departure)

  • [ ] Finalize the team roster.
  • [ ] Run a standard background check on all approved adult team members.
  • [ ] Collect copies of each team member's passport photo page. Verify that passports are valid for at least 6 months after the return date.
  • [ ] Set and communicate clear fundraising deadlines.
  • [ ] Book and confirm all team flights.
  • [ ] Arrange and confirm all in-country housing.
  • [ ] Lead the first 1-2 pre-trip meetings, focusing on spiritual foundations, team building, and the "why" of the trip.

Phase 3: Finalizing (1-2 Months Before Departure)

  • [ ] Collect signed liability waiver/release forms from each team member.
  • [ ] Collect confidential medical history forms and emergency contact information.
  • [ ] Review medical forms for any potential issues (e.g., severe allergies, chronic conditions) and create a specific emergency plan if needed. Notify on field contact of any medical needs.
  • [ ] Purchase team-wide travel insurance that includes medical emergency and evacuation coverage.
  • [ ] Lead core pre-trip training sessions (Cultural Overview, Ministry "Roadmap," Security Protocols).
  • [ ] Lead the team in establishing a "Team Covenant" to set behavioral expectations.
  • [ ] Ensure all team members have downloaded, installed, and joined the team groups on Signal and WhatsApp.

Phase 4: Launch (Final Week Before Departure)

  • [ ] Confirm final fundraising and payment status for all team members.
  • [ ] Create and distribute a final, detailed trip itinerary (flights, addresses, key phone numbers).
  • [ ] Confirm in-country transportation (e.g., airport pickup).
  • [ ] Prepare a comprehensive team first-aid kit.
  • [ ] Send a final communication email with packing reminders and airport meeting details.
  • [ ] Lead the final team meeting, focusing on prayer and commissioning.
  • [ ] Make sure all travelers have 2 photocopies of their passport.

Team Member Checklist

Phase 1: Foundation (6-12 Months Before Departure)

  • [ ] Prayerfully consider the commitment and complete the trip application by the deadline.
  • [ ] Crucial: Apply for or renew your passport. It must be valid for at least 6 months after the trip's return date. This process can take several months, so do it immediately.

Phase 2: Building (4-6 Months Before Departure)

  • [ ] Upon acceptance to the team, provide a copy of your passport photo page to the team leader.
  • [ ] Begin fundraising. Send out support letters/emails to potential partners.
  • [ ] Identify and recruit a personal prayer team of 5-10 people.
  • [ ] Attend all scheduled pre-trip meetings and begin getting to know your teammates.
  • [ ] Begin any assigned pre-trip reading (Scripture or other materials).

Phase 3: Finalizing (1-3 Months Before Departure)

  • [ ] Meet all fundraising deadlines.
  • [ ] Sign and return the liability waiver/release form.
  • [ ] Complete and submit the confidential medical history and emergency contact forms.
  • [ ] Consult your doctor regarding any necessary vaccinations or personal medications. Obtain enough medication for the entire trip, plus a few extra days.
  • [ ] Participate actively in all pre-trip training sessions. Practice the ministry tools.
  • [ ] Download, install, and test Signal and WhatsApp. Join the team groups.

Phase 4: Launch (Final Week Before Departure)

  • [ ] Notify your personal bank/credit card companies of your international travel dates to prevent holds on your accounts.
  • [ ] Plan for and acquire personal spending money (e.g., cash to exchange for Dirhams upon arrival).
  • [ ] Make 2 copies of your passport, put 1 in carry-on and 1 in checked bag.
  • [ ] Pack according to the provided list, focusing on modest attire.
  • [ ] Notify US State of your travel: https://travel.state.gov/en/international-travel/travel-advisories/smart-traveler-enrollment-program.html
  • [ ] Look up and save the US Consul number to your phone.
  • [ ] Review the final trip itinerary.
  • [ ] Send a final update to your prayer team.
  • [ ] Attend the final team prayer and commissioning meeting.
